üìö How to Use Delhi Metro Route Finder

Step 1: Select Your Stations

Choose your boarding station from the first dropdown and your destination from the second dropdown. You can search by typing the station name.

Step 2: Find Your Route

Click the "Find Best Route" button to get the fastest route with time estimates, fare calculation, and interchange information.

Step 3: Follow the Route

Follow the step-by-step directions with platform numbers. Green dots show your boarding station, multicolor dots show interchanges, and red dots show your destination.

Step 4: Additional Features

Use the swap button to reverse your journey, clear button to start over, and check crowd tips for the best travel times.

Station colors help you identify your journey progress:

  • Multicolor: Interchange stations
  • Red: Your destination station
  • Gray: Regular stations
